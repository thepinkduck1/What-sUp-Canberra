Welcome!

Canberra is the capital city of Australia, and it is the eight largest city overall. Canberra is avery good place to visit in Australia, firstly it is not as crowded as Sydney or Melbourne, so it is not very hard to travel around and there are not many traffic jams. Canberra has many important buildings and landmarks, like the Parliament house, the old parliament house and the War Memorial Museum. Canberra also has a lot of local events, like Sky fire, Anzac Day March and the Canberra Show.



But not all the events happening in Canberra are publicised and are also not known by every Canberran and tourist. The aim of this WebApp is to inform every Canberran and tourist visiting Canberra about all the amazing events that are happening in and around Canberra.
